Norwegian forest cat

General facts

  • Origin : Norway
  • Average lifespan: between 14 and 16 years
  • Appearance: triangle formed head, double fur, a tuft of hair our of both ears
  • Character: intelligent, social, tolerant, active

The Norwegian Forest Cat is strongly built and larger than an average cat. The breed has a long, sturdy body, long legs and a bushy tail. Is alert, intelligent and playful, lovely and kind. This cat loves attention, is alays in a good mood and patient with children.
