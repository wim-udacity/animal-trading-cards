Norwegian forest cat

The Norwegian Forest Cat is strongly built and larger than an average cat.

  • Origin: Norway
  • Average lifespan: 16 years
  • Appearance: triangle formed head
  • Character: intelligent, social, tolerant

The breed has a long, sturdy body, long legs and a bushy tail. Is alert, intelligent and playful, lovely and kind. This cat loves attention, is always in a good mood and patient with children.
